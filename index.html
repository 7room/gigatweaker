GIGATweaker is a free powerful and compact tool that allows you to optimize and tweak your Windows 7. Take GIGATweaker with you

GIGATweaker is completely portable and lightweight tool with built-in Startup Manager and UnInstall Manager. All changes made by GIGATweaker in the system are transparent through informative console.
